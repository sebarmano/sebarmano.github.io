Vijilent iPhone app

  • Objective-C iPhone Front End

Vijilent uses predictive analytics to give peace of mind to consumers who meet online. We search publicly available databases across the internet to calculate a V-Score—a rating system scoring users on a scale of 1-100 based on the Veracity, Verifiability, Virtue and V-fit of the individual's web presence.
